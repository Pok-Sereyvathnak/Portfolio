About Me

I am a passionate Survey Engineer with hands-on experience in construction site surveying, structural layout, and pile positioning. My work spans from setting out pile locations to grading road levels on large-scale residential and institutional projects in Cambodia.

With a Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering (Building Construction) from Norton University, I combine academic knowledge with practical field expertise to deliver accurate, reliable survey results on complex construction sites.

I thrive in dynamic environments, collaborating closely with sub-contractors and structural engineers to ensure every element of a project is executed to specification.
